HerSaathi Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Contact: her.saathi.care@gmail.com

HerSaathi is a period and wellness companion. This policy is a production draft and should be reviewed before public launch.

Information You Add

HerSaathi can store your name, age group, cycle setup, period records, symptom logs, mood check-ins, notification preferences, and AI chat history if you choose to keep it.

Local-First Storage

Your core wellness records are stored on your device first. Local records stay on your device unless you choose to export them, restore them, delete them, or enable cloud sync.

Optional Cloud Backup

If you sign in with Google and enable Firestore cloud sync, selected wellness data may be stored under your Firebase user id. You can choose which data scopes to upload from the app.

AI Processing

Common questions can be answered locally. If cloud AI is enabled, HerSaathi sends limited context such as cycle phase, recent symptoms, mood, and your question through a backend service. Full wellness records are not sent to AI unless a future feature requests clear consent.

Third-Party Services

HerSaathi may use Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Cloud Functions, Gemini, OpenAI, Expo, and Google sign-in where enabled. These services process data only for app functionality and related support.

Deletion And Control

You can export local data, delete local data, delete your Firestore backup, pause sync, sign out, or contact support to request account/cloud data deletion.

Children And Teens

HerSaathi is not intended for children under 13. Teens should use the app with guardian guidance where required by local law or family rules.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, email her.saathi.care@gmail.com.
