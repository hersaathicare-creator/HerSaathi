# HerSaathi Screenshot Plan

Google Play preview assets should show the real app experience, not marketing-only mockups.

Capture at least these:

1. Home dashboard
2. Cycle status and daily check-in
3. Track period entry
4. Symptom logging
5. Relief Mode
6. Ask AI with Saathi
7. Pragya Premium locked state
8. Reports
9. Profile with Legal & Safety / Testing Center

## Suggested Caption Copy

Use short captions if you design framed screenshots later:

```text
Understand your cycle daily
Track periods and symptoms
Check in with your mood
Use Relief Mode when needed
Ask Saathi for gentle support
Review private wellness reports
Control your data and privacy
```

## Visual Guidance

1. Use real app screens from the Android APK.
2. Keep captions short and readable.
3. Do not show fake medical claims.
4. Do not show fake guaranteed predictions.
5. Do not imply diagnosis, treatment, or emergency use.
6. Avoid showing API keys, emails other than official public contact, or private user data.
