## Full Description

```text
HerSaathi is a simple period and wellness companion designed to help you understand your body with calm, private, everyday support.

Track your cycle, log symptoms, check in with your mood, review local reports, and use Relief Mode when you are not feeling well. HerSaathi focuses on a fresh, gentle, easy-to-read experience with local-first records and clear privacy controls.

What you can do:

- Complete a simple onboarding flow
- Track your last period, cycle length, and period duration
- Log period entries with start date, end date, flow, and symptoms
- Log common symptoms such as cramps, bloating, acne, and fatigue
- Complete one daily mood check-in per day
- View your cycle day, phase, and next period estimate
- Use Relief Mode for cramps, fatigue, anxiety, and headache support
- Read care tips for hydration, food, sleep, breathing, and gentle movement
- Review local reports and wellness patterns
- Export, restore, or delete local data
- Use optional Google sign-in and Firestore cloud backup when enabled
- Use Saathi for free local answers and Gemini-backed support once cloud AI is activated
- Unlock Pragya for premium advanced actions after subscriptions are connected

Privacy and safety:

HerSaathi is local-first. Core wellness records stay on your device unless you sign in, enable cloud sync, and choose what to upload. You can delete local data from the app and request account/cloud data deletion through support.

HerSaathi is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, prescribe medicine, or replace a doctor. Cycle predictions and AI responses are wellness support only. Seek medical care for severe pain, very heavy bleeding, fainting, fever, pregnancy concerns, or symptoms that feel unusual.

Official support:
her.saathi.care@gmail.com
```
